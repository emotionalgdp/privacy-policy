Emotional GDP — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Emotional GDP ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications published by Emotional GDP, including but not limited to:

  • Ocean Blast — Ocean puzzle game

More games will be added here as we publish them.

1. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect any personal information from you. However, our third-party advertising partner, Google AdMob, may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Device information (device type, operating system version)
  • Advertising ID
  • IP address
  • App usage data and interactions
  • General location data (country/region level only)

2. How We Use Information

The information collected by Google AdMob is used to:

  • Show relevant advertisements within our apps
  • Measure ad performance and effectiveness
  • Improve the overall advertising experience
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties other than as described in this policy.

3. Third-Party Services

Our apps use Google AdMob for advertising. Google AdMob may collect and use data as described in Google's Privacy Policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting:

https://www.google.com/settings/ads

You can also opt out via your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

4. Data We Store

Our apps may store the following data locally on your device only:

  • Game progress and scores
  • Level completion status
  • App preferences and settings

This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

5. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at emotionalgdp@gmail.com and we will take steps to delete such information.

6. Data Retention

We do not store any personal data on our servers. Any data collected by Google AdMob is retained according to Google's data retention policies. Game data stored locally on your device can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of personalized ads through your device settings
  • Request information about data collected about you from Google
  • Reset or delete your advertising ID through your device settings
  • Uninstall our apps at any time to remove all locally stored data

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information in our apps. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: emotionalgdp@gmail.com

Developer: Emotional GDP

We will respond to your inquiry within 7 business days.
